









AC Behaviour rules

- Current behaviour

    - AC allows sustained peak decel at 100% brake
    - Steering does not significantly reduce longitudinal G
    - Proper braking technique is not rewarded
    - iRacing shows early peak and decay of G (Accel longitudinal in m/s^2) with steering

- Target behaviour

    - Peak decel occurs early in braking zone
    - Sustained 100% brake reduces efficiency
    - Steering reduces longitudinal G significantly
    - Proper trail braking improves performance
